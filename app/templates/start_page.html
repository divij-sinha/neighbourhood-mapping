Where are Chicago's neighborhoods?

Chicago prides itself as “The City of Neighborhoods” but where are they? Other than the 77 “Community Areas” mapped out in the 1920s, no official neighborhood boundaries exist. Chicago needs an update!

This project asks current Chicago residents to identify Chicago's neighborhoods using an interactive map to draw neighborhood boundaries.

Participation in this survey is completely voluntary. Even if you decide to participate now, you may change your mind and stop at any time. If you agree to participate, all information will be completely anonymous and confidential. Any locations provided will only be used for analysis, summarized in a general manner, and will not be linked to you or included in publications resulting from this study. If you have questions about this research study, you can contact us at urbanism@uchicago.edu.

By clicking “I agree” below, you agree that you are a resident of Chicago at least 18 years of age and consent to participate in this study voluntarily.
