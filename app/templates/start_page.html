Draw your neighborhood in less than 3 minutes!

Chicago prides itself as “The City of Neighborhoods” but where are they? Other than the 77 “Community Areas” mapped out in the 1920s, no official neighborhood boundaries exist. Chicago needs an update!

The purpose of this survey is to better understand — and therefore better support — Chicago's neighborhoods. We're asking current Chicago residents to identify Chicago's neighborhoods using an interactive map to draw neighborhood boundaries.
